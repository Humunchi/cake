PINEAPPLE CAKE🍍

INGREDIENTS🍮

  • Eggs 2
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Baking powder
  • Lemon rind of 1 lemon
  • Butter
  • Inkomazi (sour milk)
  • Pineapple fresh is better

STEPS👣

  1. preheat oven to 170C
  2. butter baking tray, dust with sugar and cinnamon then spread pineapple slices on top
  3. mix remaining butter, sugar, egg yolks, lemon rind until smoothy textured
  4. whip egg whites with vanilla essence till smooth
  5. combine both egg mixtures, flour ,salt, baking powder. You should have an airy mixture
  6. spoon the batter into the tray prepared in step 2
  7. bake for 35 to 40 minutes till an object poked into the cake comes out dry indicating that the cake has baked to the centre
